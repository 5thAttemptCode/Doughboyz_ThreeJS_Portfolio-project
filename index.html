Doughboyz are in the building


Doughboyz is a sensuous combination of flour, water and salt, mixed with passion and unconditional love to sourdough bread. The main part of our excellent bread is the house's own sour root, thanks to which the crumb is perfectly fluffy, tasty and just right in the mouth. In addition to the crusty bottom, it also lifts the mood!

What it is all about


The difference between a good and a great bread starts with the quality of grains. You only get out what you put in. It doesn't matter if you add raisins and nuts, or spices and seeds to your dough. A mediocre flour can never become an excellent dough, and if its not excellent, whats the point? Our carefully prepared and elevated sourdough base offers a culinary experience, both with fillings or simply on its own.

Let's break bread


On the first Saturday of each month we have a little bake-off in our backyard. Sourdough pizza, bread samples, new sourdough pastry-creations, great wine and local beer; all for a small Dollar. Everyone is welcome, first come first serve! Just drop in and have a good time. And YES: we do put pinapple on our pizza!!! It's the right thing to do!!!
